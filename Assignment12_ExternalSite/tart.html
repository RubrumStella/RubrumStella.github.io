About the pastry

A frangipane tart is a classic French dessert consisting of a crisp pastry shell filled with frangipane—a rich, velvety, and nutty almond cream custard. It is typically made with butter, sugar, eggs, and ground almonds, often baked with fruits like pears, raspberries, or apricots. The filling bakes into a thickened, cake-like consistency that is creamy, fragrant, and light.
