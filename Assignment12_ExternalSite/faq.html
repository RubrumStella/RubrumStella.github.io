Commonly asked Questions and their Answers

Q: Do you have gluten free options?

A: For this we offer alternatives that are gluten-free versions to our traditonal baked goods. Some of which include specialized cakes, breakfast pastries, and artisan breads. These options typically use alternative flours such as rice, almond, coconut, or buckwhat to that the texture of the product isn't compromised but still allows for those who require the substitution to be able to get what they were looking for.



Q: Do you have vegan or vegetarian options?

A: We do offer vegan and vegetarian products for multiple of our products including but not limited to our chocolate cakes, muffins, and cookies, but also our danishes, and some of our other desserts and pastries. Along with this some of these are also egg-free and dairy-free for our vegan customers.



Q: Do you have dairy free options?

A: Yes, we do offer dairy-free options with our alternatives being soy milk, oat milk, almond milk, and coconut milk. These can be found for most of our baked goods, but might have a few limited that aren't capable of making using an alternative.



Q: Are you able to make specific allergy substitutions?

A: While we can make special allergy substitutions we do require prior knowledge to be able to make the baked good you are looking to get on time for when you need it and do not make anything new on the spot for walk-ins.



Q: Are all of our baked goods made in house?

A: Yes, we do make all of our baked goods in house. Typically that morning and as products run out we make a new batch until a certain point, around two hours before we close, so that we don't have too many things leftover so we don't have many things that we are forced to throw away.



Q: What do you do with all of your baked goods that are left at the end of the day?

A: We give away what is left that is fit for human consumption to our local food bank so that they are able to distibute these baked goods to where they are able as well as going to our local senior center and donating some of our baked goods there as well.



Q: Where are your products sourced from?

A: We source our products from local farmers and markets that are getting their products from traceable lines so that we are sure that all of the products we but are all ethically sourced.
