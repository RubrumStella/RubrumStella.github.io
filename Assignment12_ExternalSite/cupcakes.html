About the pastry

These ultra-moist and rich Chocolate Chip Birthday Cupcakes are a really simple dessert option for your next birthday celebration! They have an irresistibly tender crumb that is moist, they are rich in chocolate flavor and with chocolate frosting on top with some fun sprinkles as decoration.
